## üîµ O-1A REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

```markdown
**REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS**

The supporting documentation for an O-1A petition must include evidence that the Beneficiary has:
- A major internationally recognized award (such as Nobel Prize), OR
- At least **THREE (3) of the following EIGHT (8)** forms of evidence:

(i) **Awards or Prizes** - Nationally or internationally recognized
(ii) **Membership** - In associations requiring outstanding achievements
(iii) **Published Material** - About the beneficiary in professional/major media
(iv) **Judging** - Of the work of others in the same or allied field
(v) **Original Contributions** - Of major significance in the field
(vi) **Scholarly Articles** - In professional or major trade publications
(vii) **Critical/Essential Capacity** - For organizations with distinguished reputation
(viii) **High Salary** - Commanded or will command high remuneration
```

## üü° EB-1A REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS

**Note**: EB-1A uses I-140 petition (not I-129), but similar brief structure

```markdown
**REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS**

**8 CFR ¬ß 204.5(h)** - Extraordinary Ability

The petition must include evidence that the alien has:
- A one-time achievement (major internationally recognized award like Nobel Prize), OR
- At least **THREE (3) of the following TEN (10)** criteria:

1. **Awards or Prizes** - Lesser nationally/internationally recognized
2. **Membership** - Requiring outstanding achievements
3. **Published Material** - About the person in professional/major media
4. **Judging** - Of the work of others
5. **Original Contributions** - Of major significance
6. **Scholarly Articles** - By the beneficiary
7. **Artistic Exhibitions/Showcases** - Display of work
8. **Leading/Critical Role** - For organizations with distinguished reputation
9. **High Salary** - Relative to others in field
10. **Commercial Success** - In performing arts

**Two-Step Kazarian Analysis:**
1. **Step 1**: Does petitioner meet at least 3 of 10 criteria?
2. **Step 2**: Does totality of evidence demonstrate sustained national/international acclaim and top of field?
```

## üéØ IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS FOR DOCUMENT 4 GENERATOR

### The Prompt Must:

1. **Select Correct Template** based on visa type:
   - O-1A: 8 criteria + comparable evidence
   - O-1B: 6 criteria + comparable evidence
   - P-1A: 7 criteria + NO comparable evidence + itinerary
   - EB-1A: 10 criteria + comparable evidence + Kazarian analysis

2. **Include ALL Sections** in order:
   - Petitioner Employment Structure
   - Prior Approval Deference (if applicable)
   - About the Petitioner
   - About the Beneficiary
   - Third Party Employers (O/P only)
   - Consultation Letter (O/P only)
   - Regulatory Requirements
   - Criterion-by-Criterion Analysis
   - Final Merits Determination (EB-1A only)
   - Exhibit List

3. **For Each Criterion Being Claimed:**
   - Start with checkbox (Yes/No)
   - Quote exact regulatory language
   - List evidence with exhibit numbers
   - Provide 1-2 page legal analysis
   - Reference sources from Doc 3 with tier ratings from Doc 2
   - Include comparable evidence section if applicable
   - Conclude with strength assessment

4. **Cross-Reference Documents:**
   - Pull source credibility from Document 2
   - Pull statistical analysis from Document 1
   - Pull URL references from Document 3
   - Maintain exhibit numbering consistency

5. **Professional Legal Tone:**
   - Formal but persuasive
   - Cite CFR sections correctly
   - Use case law where appropriate
   - Conclude each criterion strongly

6. **Exhibit Organization:**
   - Group by criterion
   - Include tier ratings
   - Reference source URLs
   - Note credibility assessments
